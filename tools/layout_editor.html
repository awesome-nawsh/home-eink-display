E-ink layout editor

800 × 480 — Waveshare 7.5″ B/W/R

Elements

    Select an element to edit its position and size.
    Drag boxes to move, drag the bottom-right corner to resize. Shift/Cmd/Ctrl-click (canvas or list) to add/remove from the selection, or drag a box on empty canvas to marquee-select — drag any selected box to move the whole group together. Grid snaps to 5px. Boxes are colored by which image buffer they draw to (black vs red layer), matching the physical display. Dashed boxes are text/labels — their real footprint depends on rendered text, not the box size.

    Sleep screen

    Not a coordinate layout. Outside the wake window, display_sleep_screen() fetches a full-canvas screenshot of your Home Assistant dashboard (via a Puppeteer add-on), converts it to grayscale, and pushes it straight to the black buffer. The red buffer stays blank white — there's nothing to position or recolor here.

    Driven by (.env)

    SLEEP_SCREEN_DASHBOARD — HA Lovelace view URL
    SLEEP_SCREEN_EINK_MODE — 2 = B/W/R viewport hint
    SLEEP_SCREEN_ZOOM, SLEEP_SCREEN_WAIT — render zoom / delay (ms)
    SLEEP_SCREEN_THEME — HA theme activated before capture
